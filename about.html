About Me

My parents always scolds me for breaking stuffs. Back in 2005-2008, we had a hunch back desktop computer. My curiosity to know what that thing is really made up of and how all this magical software inside of it jumps around with just a mouse click, led me to hurribly decouple and disect our computer machine. I want to hold those softwares in my hand and let them jump around in my hand (Quite funny though).

One thing about me is that scolding never stops me from getting more curious. My curiosity even made me to learn how to stand strong and withstand any form of scolding from my parents. At least they are proud of me now, because my curiosity led to a vast range of knowledge. I have learnt more about computer (Both hardware and software), and how to create softwares. My parents are always like, 'If we knew this is what you did become, we would have bought more computers for you to spoil.'

My main focus these days is contributing to or developing open source projects that would be of great use to developers making use of Kivy framework. What really inspired me to do so is that most of my personal projects needs them too and no one was making any move to do so, so I took it upon myself to do so. You can check some of them out here.

When I'm not on my computer, I would be cracking my mum or sister up with lots of jokes. Sometimes I play video games or hangout with my friends and talk all day. Yea I talk a lot when I'm not on my computer.
